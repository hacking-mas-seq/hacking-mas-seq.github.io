Krona Plot Navigation Page

Below are links to interactive plots made from the same data as our poster Figures 2A and 2B.

Krona plots are interactive visualizations that can be essentially thought of as multi-layered pie charts for hierarchical data. They are similar to our poster's sunburst plots, however you can choose your taxonomic resolution and zoom in on a specific region of interest. Please feel free to explore!
